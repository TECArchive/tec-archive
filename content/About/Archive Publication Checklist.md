# Token Engineering Commons (2019–2025) Archive Publication Checklist

This checklist defines the minimum conditions for publishing the TEC archive in a way that is durable, credible, and final.

For the canonical checklist (and the enforcement target for data publication), see:

- https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/blob/main/PUBLICATION_CHECKLIST.md

## Publication Declaration

Once the checklist is satisfied and the archive is released:

- The archive is historical, not operational
- Changes are not expected or implied
- TEC exists solely as a public record
