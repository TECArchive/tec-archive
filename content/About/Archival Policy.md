# Archival Policy

This page defines what we collect, what we publish, and how we handle privacy.

## Principles

- Minimize harm: avoid doxxing, harassment, or unwanted amplification
- Prefer consent where possible
- Keep verifiable references to primary sources

## In scope (initial)

- Governance-relevant channels/categories
- Public announcements
- Onchain governance + treasury transactions

## Out of scope (initial)

- Direct messages
- Private channels
- Personal data (emails, phone numbers, addresses)
