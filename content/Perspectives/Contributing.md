# Contributing a Perspective

Thank you for adding your voice to the record. See [[Perspectives]] for what this section is and the consent/curation terms.

## Two ways to submit

1. **Open an issue (easiest).** In the archive's GitHub repository, click **New Issue → "Share a Perspective"**, and fill in the form. A steward will add it here.
2. **Open a pull request.** Copy the template below into a new file at `content/Perspectives/<a short title>.md`, fill it in, and open a PR.

## Template

```markdown
---
title: <a short title for your reflection>
---

# <your title>

**By:** <your name, a handle, or "anonymous">
**Role / era in the TEC:** <optional — e.g. "Gravity WG, 2021–2022">

<your reflection, in your own words>
```

## A few guidelines

- Write in your own voice. Critique is welcome; this section exists for honest reflection.
- Keep it to your own experience. Please don't share other people's private information or contact details.
- Length is up to you — a paragraph or several pages are both fine.
- By submitting, you consent to public, attributed publication as described in [[Perspectives]].
