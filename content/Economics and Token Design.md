# Economics & Token Design

TEC's economic system was its most ambitious technical artifact: a token economy designed to fund public goods sustainably, launched through a community fundraising event and governed by its participants. This section indexes the economic and token-design record.

## The Hatch

TEC's economic birth. The Hatch was simultaneously a fundraising event and a governance-formation process: backers contributed wxDai and builders converted Impact Hours, together minting Hatch tokens and forming the Hatch DAO. The source material includes extensive Hatch parameter design — goals, period, tribute, vesting, Impact Hour rate, and funding-pool assumptions — plus test hatches, "parameter parties," and live Hatch data and rehearsals.

See [[Overview/Tokens of the TEC]] for the token types involved.

## The Augmented Bonding Curve (ABC)

After the Commons Upgrade (January 2022), the TEC token's price and supply were governed by an Augmented Bonding Curve feeding a common pool. The archive preserves bonding-curve models, simulation work (including "what-if" matrices and proposals tested with large reserves), and a later Bonding Curve Research Group that studied its tradeoffs.

## Praise, Impact Hours, and rewards

A defining cultural-economic system: **Praise** recognized contribution, which translated into **Impact Hours** and later into token rewards. The record includes Praise quantification proposals and data, reward-board requirements and guidelines, SourceCred experiments, contributor-factor simulations, and the recurring rewards rounds — along with the difficult mid-2022 debate over fairness across paid, unpaid, and Praise-based contributors.

## Treasury and grants

As TEC matured, attention shifted to **treasury stewardship** (diversification into ETH, wstETH, sDAI, and reserve strategy) and to a structured **grants program** (Small Grants pilot and Grants v1 in 2023, multiple TE Grants rounds, and Token Engineering the Superchain).

## Sunset

The economic story closes with the 2025 **sunsetting** process: a token infrastructure and utility report, a sunsetting framework, an OTC buyback proposal, the shutdown framework, and the final **TEC Token Sunset** in December 2025. See the [[Timeline]].
