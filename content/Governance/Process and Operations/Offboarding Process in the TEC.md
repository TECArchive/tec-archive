# Offboarding Process in the TEC

**What it was.** The humane, structured process for a contributor to step back or leave — designed so people could take time off or transition out without dropping commitments on the rest of the community.

**Who it was for.** Any contributor rotating out (temporarily or permanently), and especially Community Stewards managing role transitions.

**What it looked like.** A staged process: give notice (via the Gravity typeform or to a Steward/Graviton, ideally 1–2 weeks ahead), confirm a transition date at the next Stewards call, and hand off work during sprint planning so priorities are reassigned without overloading others. It paired the mechanics with a culture of "give a lot of praise along the way," and gave special handling to replacing Community Steward roles.
