# Reward Board — Requirements and Guidelines

**What it was.** The charter for the **Reward Board** — the small group of trusted long-term contributors that kept the TEC's contribution-reward system fair, functioning, and transparent.

**Who it was for.** Reward Board members and the Quantifiers they managed, plus the wider community relying on transparent reward distributions.

**What it looked like.** A responsibilities-and-permissions document: request funds from the Common Pool for reward distributions, manage the Quantifier pool and quantification periods, review and approve/reject distributions, tune the reward system's settings, and publish transparent reports. It listed the on-chain actions available to members inside the Reward Board's Aragon DAO, connecting the [[Praise]] culture to the treasury.
