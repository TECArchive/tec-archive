# Working Groups

For much of its operating life the TEC organized labor through **working groups** — semi-autonomous teams with their own manifestos, agendas, budgets, and rhythms. This subsection has a page for each documented working group, describing what it did, what it accomplished, and where to find its source documents in the [data repository](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/tree/main/documents). For the broader narrative — how the working-group model worked and how it gave way to a Coordination Team in late 2022 — see [[Working Groups Overview]].

## The groups

- [[Community Stewards Working Group]] — community health, stewardship, and operations
- [[Gravity Working Group]] — conflict transformation and mediation
- [[Rewards Working Group]] — the contribution-reward system
- [[Legal Working Group]] — legal strategy and risk
- [[Transparency Working Group]] — transparency practices, reporting, and audits
- [[Tech Spec Working Group]] — technical specification of the Commons
- [[Hatch Outreach Working Group]] — recruiting and supporting hatchers before launch
- [[Communitas Working Group]] — community-building and belonging

Other groups active in the record — Communications / Commons Swarm, WG Omega (research), Soft Gov, and Params — are covered in [[Working Groups Overview]] and their materials appear in the corresponding data-repository folders.
