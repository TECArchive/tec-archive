# Gravity Working Group

**Function.** Gravity was the TEC's **conflict-transformation and mediation system** — the working group responsible for interpersonal health, dispute resolution, and building the community's capacity to handle conflict constructively. It treated conflict not as a failure but as a normal, workable part of communal life.

**What it accomplished.** Gravity built a full practice around mediation: a manifesto, **Graviton training** programs, mediation and observation forms, self-reflection processes, and an extensive set of call notes. It became influential enough to spin outward as a wider "Gravity DAO" effort serving other communities, and it issued POAPs for its trainings and events. Its tooling is referenced in [[Code and Technical Artifacts]].

**Documents & resources.** Gravity's manifesto and training materials are in the data repository under **Gravity WG** — [browse the folder](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/tree/main/documents/Gravity%20WG) or download the [Gravity WG bundle](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/raw/main/bundles/Gravity-WG.zip). *Note:* the most consent-sensitive records (running mediation call notes and case-specific mediation/observation forms) are **withheld** from the public release per the [[Archival Policy]].
