# Legal Working Group

**Function.** The Legal WG handled the TEC's **legal strategy and risk** — navigating the uncertain regulatory terrain around a token-issuing DAO, and advising the community on structure, compliance, and liability.

**What it accomplished.** It produced a manifesto and legal strategy, ran a legal audit of the organization, and maintained ongoing terms work (including the [[Proposal Terms]] and platform disclaimers). Its work underpinned decisions from the Hatch through the sunset, where legal considerations shaped how the treasury could be distributed.

**Documents & resources.** The group's manifesto is in the data repository under **Legal WG** — [browse the folder](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/tree/main/documents/Legal%20WG) or download the [Legal WG bundle](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/raw/main/bundles/Legal-WG.zip). *(The legal audit spreadsheet is part of the broader document release.)*
