# Communitas Working Group

**Function.** Communitas focused on **community-building and belonging** — the sense of shared identity and connection that held the TEC together beyond its formal structures. ("Communitas" names exactly that feeling of communal bond.)

**What it accomplished.** It ran community-building initiatives and guide/onboarding support during the TEC's active period. Like several groups, it was formally wound down as the organization consolidated, and its **retirement document** records that transition.

**Documents & resources.** The group's retirement record is in the data repository under **Communitas WG** — [browse the folder](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/tree/main/documents/Communitas%20WG) or download the [Communitas WG bundle](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/raw/main/bundles/Communitas-WG.zip).
