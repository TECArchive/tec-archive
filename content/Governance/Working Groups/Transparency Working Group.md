# Transparency Working Group

**Function.** The Transparency WG made the TEC's inner workings legible — recording and publishing community calls, running audits of the working groups, and maintaining the reporting and decision-tracking that turned a busy DAO into a reviewable public record.

**What it accomplished.** It stewarded the TEC's [[Sources/YouTube|YouTube]] channel and call recordings, ran a recurring transparency-audit program across the working groups, maintained decision-tracking and analytics, and produced the community-facing "Transparency in DAOs" research. Much of what makes this archive possible — the habit of recording and publishing — traces back to this group.

**Documents & resources.** The group's retirement record and audit materials are in the data repository under **Transparency WG** — [browse the folder](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/tree/main/documents/Transparency%20WG) or download the [Transparency WG bundle](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/raw/main/bundles/Transparency-WG.zip).
