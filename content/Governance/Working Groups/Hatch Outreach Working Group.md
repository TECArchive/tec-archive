# Hatch Outreach Working Group

**Function.** The Hatch Outreach WG (part of the launch-era effort) **recruited and supported the Hatchers** — the members of the Trusted Seed who would contribute to the Hatch — and onboarded newcomers into the community ahead of launch.

**What it accomplished.** It ran outreach and onboarding for the Hatch: a manifesto, agenda notes, onboarding journeys, and outreach audits tracking who was being brought in and supported. Its work directly shaped the community that formed around the [[The Hatch|Hatch]], which raised 1.57M wxDAI — nearly double its minimum goal.

**Documents & resources.** The group's manifesto and outreach materials are in the data repository under **Hatch Outreach WG** — [browse the folder](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/tree/main/documents/Hatch%20Outreach%20WG) or download the [Hatch Outreach WG bundle](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/raw/main/bundles/Hatch-Outreach-WG.zip).
