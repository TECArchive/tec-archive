# Rewards Working Group

**Function.** The Rewards WG ran the TEC's **contribution-reward system** — the machinery that turned recognition of work into token rewards. It managed the Praise pipeline, the Quantifier pool, and the Reward Board that approved and disbursed distributions.

**What it accomplished.** It operationalized the TEC's defining cultural mechanism: members gave [[Praise]] for contributions, Quantifiers scored it, and the Reward Board swapped Common Pool funds to `$TEC` and distributed rewards on a regular cadence — publishing transparent reports each round. Over time it iterated on quantification methods and reward-system parameters, and codified the [[Reward Board - Requirements and Guidelines]].

**Documents & resources.** The group's manifesto, notes, and reward-board guidelines are in the data repository under **Rewards WG** — [browse the folder](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/tree/main/documents/Rewards%20WG) or download the [Rewards WG bundle](https://github.com/TECArchive/tec-archive-data/raw/main/bundles/Rewards-WG.zip).
