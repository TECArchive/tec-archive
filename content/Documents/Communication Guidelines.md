## Internal Comms

### Weekly Syncs | Thursday 8pm CET

* Thursday calls are for syncing, questions and removing blockers for the work happening async. It’s expected that new proposals and action items will come out of each call, creating a streamline for the roadmap execution.
* The call agenda is shared in the TE Commons Discord chat in advance.

### [Advice Process](https://corporate-rebels.com/advice-process) Communication

* When sharing the proposal on Discord, the doc owner should tag the individuals they need advice from.
- Chosen advisors can opt out of the advice process if they wish by just telling the document owner or they can delegate their role to someone else.
- The ones chosen to give input in a certain proposal are encouraged to make comments directly in the doc. The TEC Discord Channel is an acceptable place for questions and discussions, but preferably feedback will be inside the proposal to facilitate integration and a wholesome view of everyone's inputs.
- PM’s can be a friendly and gentle reminder to gather remaining feedback and are used only as a last resource to contact someone.
