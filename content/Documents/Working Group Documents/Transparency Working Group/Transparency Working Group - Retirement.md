**Transparency Working Group Retirement**

**Description & Deliverable**

Retirement Date
	The Transparency working group retired on October 31st. We as a Commons have built different practices that facilitate transparency and mutual monitoring in the community and have been utilizing tools such as Dework, Dune and the ABC Advanced page. With these tools, there is no need for transparency being a WG  in the TEC. Milestones and salaries can be tracked using Dework, while the audit data can be automated using Dework as well. All other operational tasks will be maintained initially by Sampo WG, but the TEC restructuration proposal will define where those tasks will fall in.

WG Learnings

* Transparency stewards are not “police” who should impose transparency across the community, but provide guidance on how members can be transparent with their actions and decisions.
* The goal of transparency is not only gathering relevant information around decisions being made, processes, fund management, etc. but also making it accessible for the  members to [REDACTED] mutual monitoring
