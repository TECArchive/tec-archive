# **Reward Board Requirements and Guidelines**

*The Reward Board is the group of indispensable long-term contributors that will make sure the Reward System remains fair, functioning and transparent*

## **What are the Reward Board's responsibilities?**

The Reward Board has a few important tasks to make sure that the Reward System works and is effective:

* Request funds from the TEC Common Pool for reward distributions, swap to TEC
* Add Quantifiers and manage the Quantifier Pool, intialize Quantification Periods
* Final review of Quants with Quantifiers
* Approve or Reject then (if Approved) disburse reward distributions
* Analyze and adjust the settings of the Reward System
* Publish transparent reports of reward distributions and changes to Reward System settings to the TEC community

Within the Reward Board Aragon DAO, members have a few on-chain actions available to them:

* Approve or reject reward distributions
* Approve the Aragon Agent (the DAO, basically) to swap wxDAI for TEC
* Mint tokens for new members
* Burn tokens for inactive members

## **Okay, what do I need to do exactly?**

In this section we'll break down each of the responsiblites defined above, of the Reward Board

### **Request funds from the TEC Common Pool for reward distributions, convert them into TEC**

The Reward Board will need TEC tokens in its own treasury (on the Aragon DAO) to be able to then disburse them to Reward System participants. You'll need to create a funding request for wxDAI on the TEC gardens via Conviction Voting. It would be wise to request enough funds to keep a constant float in the treasury, avoiding running out of funds to distribute. This amount could vary by current Conviction Voting settings and the amount of funds currently in the Common Pool. Discussing between other Members is key.

You'll have to justify the amount you request to the community in order for them to vote to approve your request. Conviction Voting takes time, so budget for that as well. You can use previous distribution data to back up your funding requests.

Once the funds are approved and received into the Reward Board treasury they will need to be converted from wxDAI to TEC. The Aragon Agent will need to be used to facilitating actually swapping the tokens in the treasury. The Reward Board can use either the Augmented Bonding Curve or secondary markets (Sushiswap, Honeyswap) to acquire TEC.

### **Add Quantifiers and manage the Quantifier Pool, intialize Quantification Periods**

In the Reward System dashboard you as an Admin will need to add successfully onboarded Quantifers into the Quantifier Pool. From this pool, Quantifiers are randomly selected for "Quant Duty", you will not be able to direclty control who is selected to quantify Praise. You will however need to make sure that there are a healthy amount of Quantifiers in the Pool so that:

1. It's not constantly the same participants who are quantifying praise
2. The workload is reasonable for Quantifiers to complete in a timely manner. (i.e the amount of Praise each Quantifier needs to quantify)

The final task will be to launch Quantification Periods, this is done manually via the Reward System Dashboard. You will need to specify the dates for the Quantification Period, generate the Quantifier list for that round and then open the Quantification Period.

### **Final review of Quants with Quantifiers**

Near the end of every Quant, when Quantifiers have completed their assigned praise, a synchronous review will take place between all the Quantifiers and a few Reward Board members to review and adjust any quantifications of Praise. At the end of this review the final Quant proposal should be sent to the Reward Board DAO for approval.

### **Approving or rejecting then sending reward distributions**

Quantification cycles happen every two weeks, we collect data from Sourcecred and Praise, Quantifiers.. quantify and the Reward Board analyzes the results.

As a Reward Board Member you will have 5 days to review, modify (if necessary), propose, pass and disburse rewards to TEC contributors.

Modifying a reward distribution is not for the purpose of minor tweaks but rather for correcting considerably disproportionate distribution resulting from flaws in the system or gamification from system participants (Contributors, Quantifiers, Reward Board). Identifying flaws or improvements ties in with the next responsibility.

### **Analyzing and adjusting the settings of the Reward System**

Reviewing the Quants in depth will help you make data-driven proposals to alter any of these possible settings:

* SoureCred weight configurations
* Praise quantification weights (i.e the Fibonacci sequence numbers)
* The relational value between SourceCred and Praise
* How much TEC is distributed per quant
* The relative amount that Contributors, Quantifiers and the Reward Board receive from each distribution.
* Approval or support required voting settings of the Reward Board DAO

Be aware any changes to these settings will need to be communicated to the community, which flows into the following task:

### **Publishing transparent reports of Quants and changes to Reward System settings to the TEC community**

For every Quant we'll need to produce a report showing where TEC funds were sent. This will be a simple forum post in the Reward Reports section of the TEC forum. This report will only need to contain the name of the recipient (Discord Handle?Twitter Handle?) and how much TEC they received.

For changing settings to the Reward System you'll have to create a forum post in the same Reward Reports section that clearly explains what setting was changed and why.

## **How do I join the Reward Board?**

Anyone can apply to join the Reward Board, all you need to do is create a vote on the DAO to mint yourself a membership token. Your vote must pass the current approval and support required settings (enough current members have to vote yes, basically). The Reward Board is tasked with distributing rewards to TEC contributors, ***punctually***. Before applying, consider if you have the time and bandwidth necessary to fulfill the responsibilities of the Reward Board in a timely manner.

Favorable qualities of Reward Board Members would demonstrate these qualities:

* Desire to contribute long-term
* Detail, analytics and data oriented
* Can respond quickly (\<24 hours) to cast votes on the DAO
* Able to relay information transparently and produce transparency reports for the community

## **Can I be removed from the Reward Board?**

If you fail to cast your vote on 3 consecutive reward distribution votes (~6 weeks of inactivity) then you will be notified of an incoming vote to burn your membership token. If the burn vote passes you will no longer be able to vote on rewards distributions. Don't worry though! You can still participate in the WG, attend calls and engage in discussions! ;)
