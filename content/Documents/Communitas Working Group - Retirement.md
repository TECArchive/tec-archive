**Communitas Working Group Retirement Document**

**Retirement Date**
The Communitas Working Group was officially phased out as of October 31, 2022.  All documents and channels associated with the Communitas WG will be archived and remain on Discord, and our Gitbook.

**WG Learnings**

	People & Onboarding

* Onboarding takes time, and the Communitas WG has proposed many pathways for onboarding new members into the TEC.
* Onboarding sessions were designed for internal operations of the TEC, and provided valuable context for how members can participate.
* The amount of information needed to be conveyed during the onboarding process was immense, and requires dedicated individuals to lead these initiatives.
* Engagement was extremely successful when taking into account the reason(s) potential members were joining the TEC.  Onboarding is a good method for understanding potential stakeholders, and connecting them with the right groups of people.
* Onboarding services such as with the TE Academy is difficult for integration.
* Communitas Guides provided a proactive method of engagement that allowed for new members to become immersed within the TEC.

  Events & Engagement

* TEC events were largely successful, but need to have dedicated leads to ensure interest and engagement.
* TEC Events should continue and become more frequent as they fulfill one aspect of becoming a schelling point.
* Tracking Discord Data on TEC members was successful, and provided insights on participation within the community.  Monitoring and extracting information from this data is burdensome.
* TEC Lounge and weekly calls provided opportunities for potential new members to engage with existing community members.

**Tasks & Functions**

The Tasks & Functions provided by the Communitas Working Group are numerous, as the WG provided community events, continuous informational insight towards community engagement, maintained our informational infrastructure, welcomed newcomers and provided updates to all TEC stakeholders.  A comprehensive list of tasks and functions the Communitas WG have provided are listed below:

* Onboarding:
  * **Welcoming in Discord**
  * Orientation calls
  * TEC Lounge
  * Onboarding assistance to Token Engineering Academy
  * TEC Education
  * Onboarding Logistics (account setup, etc.)
  * **Gitbook Management**
  * Orientation Deck
  * Polling
* Professional development
  * Clear tasks for new contributors
  * Assistance completing beginner bounties
  * Matching Areas of interest chosen with WGs and tasks
  * Interest Mapping
* Social development
  * Workshops
  * **Holding Community Events**
  * **Initial Community Offerings**
* Community building
  * **Discord Management**
  * **Outreach for important community wide decisions**
  * WG’s updates

As we move forward with the TEC Transition process, we will be transferring several of these functions (in bold) to our Core Unit.  Ownership of these functions will be provided in this document at a later date.
