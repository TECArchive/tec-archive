# Telegram

**Platform:** Telegram — community channels and groups

Telegram served as a lighter-weight broadcast and chat layer alongside [[Sources/Discord]] and the [[Sources/Discourse]] forum. It was used for announcements and informal community conversation, and reached members who followed TEC primarily through Telegram.

## Role in TEC

- **Announcements** — broadcast updates for the wider community, including during the Hatch and major governance moments.
- **Informal chat** — community discussion and questions outside the structured working-group spaces.

## What the archive captures

Public broadcast channels are the priority for reference. Informal group chat is treated cautiously: it is summarized only where it documents something not otherwise recorded, and never republished verbatim where privacy is a concern.

## Scope and privacy

Out of scope: private groups, direct messages, and personal data. Personal names are redacted per the [[About/Archival Policy]]. Channel/group identifiers and export method will be recorded with the dataset exports.
